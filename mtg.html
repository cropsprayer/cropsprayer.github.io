The Master Test Gauge is a self-contained unit which runs directly from a 12v vehicle power outlet. Everything needed to test a pressure gauge is contained within this rugged metal flight case.

The case also offers a padded storage space to safely store replacement gauges. Everything you need in one place to stay ahead of the game when you are testing. The class one test gauge has been custom designed for agricultural sprayer testing.

The box opens and you are able to test the pressure gauge in front of your customer. The gauges are also tested in a vertical plane to adhere to NSTS requirements.


 The face is calibrated in 0.1 bar increments and has easy to read markings. Each Master Gauge has been calibrated to internationally recognised standards and includes an identification plaque and calibration certificate.
