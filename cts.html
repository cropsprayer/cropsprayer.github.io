The catchment system is made from strong and durable polyethylene is is the same material used by most sprayer manufacturers for the sprayer tanks.

The trays and rotationally molded, this ensures the all trays are made to the same quality.  Each tray is very light but at the same time very strong, you can even stand on an upturned tray 

Each tray has a molded lip, this lip interlocks with the next tray to ensure and leak free joint. The locking trays can be arranged so suit sprayers big or small, including off set booms. 18 trays is enough for a 24 meter spray boom
